Abstract:
> In this talk I will give an introduction to optimal transport, starting with the classic problem of Gaspard Monge. I will then present Kanatorovich's relaxation and the corresponding dual problem, which enabled today's analytic framework. After discussing the analytic foundation, I will review recent advances in computational optimal transport - in particular entropic regularisation and Sinkhorn algorithm. Finally, I will give you a very brief glimpse into my own research on inverse optimal transport, which focuses on developing tools to infer underlying transport costs from observations of optimal transportation plans.
