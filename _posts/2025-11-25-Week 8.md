Abstract:
> K-Theory has seen rapid progress over recent years and beautifully links many areas of mathematics. However, the difficult and esoteric nature of the subject makes it hard to grasp and comprehend, especially for undergraduates. I think this is a shame. I am therefore motivated to give a talk that attempts to convey the intuition of the subject, the tools necessary to study the subject, and a survey of problems/results that hopefully give a flavour of research within the subject. If by the end of the talk you walk away with an idea of what K-Theory is, I would have achieved my goal!
